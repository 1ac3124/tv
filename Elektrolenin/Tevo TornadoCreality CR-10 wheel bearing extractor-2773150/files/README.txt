# Summary

###For extraction of beariong out of the wheel

place things on screw like this:
- washer
- extractor body
- wheel
- plunger
- washer
- nut

Tighten the screw until the bearing falls into the extractor body. 

###For pressing the bearing into the wheel
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
place things on screw like this:
- washer
- extractor body
- wheel
- bearing
- plunger
- washer
- nut

Gently tighten the screw until the bearing hit the limiter inside of the wheel. Then repeat with the other side not forgetting to put between the bearings the brass washer.
